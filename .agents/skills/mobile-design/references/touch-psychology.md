# Touch Psychology

## Fitts' Law for touch
- Desktop uses a precise cursor; mobile uses a finger with a large contact area.
- Minimum touch target: 44pt (iOS) / 48dp (Android).
- Keep 8-12px spacing between adjacent targets.

## Thumb zone (one-hand use)
Top of screen = hard to reach
Middle = okay to reach
Bottom = easy to reach

Place primary actions in the easy reach zone.

## Gestures
- Never rely on gesture-only interactions.
- Always provide a visible button alternative.

## Cognitive load
- Users are often interrupted; keep tasks short.
- No hover states; make affordances visible.
- Avoid dense layouts; prefer progressive disclosure.
