# Mobile Design Thinking

## Anti-memorization prompts
- Restate the user goal, audience, and top 3 constraints.
- Identify primary context of use (on-the-go, seated, one-hand, offline).
- Identify platform(s), framework, and target device sizes.
- Identify the most important action per screen.

## Mobile is not a small desktop
- Attention is fragmented and time is limited.
- Touch is imprecise; there is no hover.
- Viewports are small and vertical by default.
- Battery, CPU, memory, and network are constrained.

## Design for worst conditions
- Bright sun and glare
- Weak or intermittent network
- One-hand usage
- Low battery and thermal throttling
